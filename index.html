Asian American Aviators

Who we are? What we do? Why we are here?

We are Asian American Pilots. We are here to encourage Asian Americans across the nation to become a part of the aviation community.

"If you build it, they will come! If they don't, then we will show up together."

Come join our club today! For more information, contact us!
